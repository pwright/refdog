Claim

XXX A verifiable assertion of permission to link to a remote site. A claim contains the URL and secret of a previous grant.

Spec properties

  • url string, required

    The URL at which the claim is redeemed.

  • secret string, required

  • ca string, required

Status properties

  • claimed boolean

    Whether the claim has been claimed.

    Suggest redeemed. "Whether the claim has been redeemed."
